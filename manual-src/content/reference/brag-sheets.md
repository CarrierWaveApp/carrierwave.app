Brag sheets and share cards are branded image cards that visualize your operating activity for sharing on social media, with friends, or for personal records.

## Overview

Carrier Wave generates several types of share cards:

- **Activation brag sheets** - Summarize a POTA/SOTA/WWFF activation
- **Activity share cards** - Summarize a day's operating activity
- **Session share cards** - Summarize any logging session
- **Weekly/monthly/all-time brag sheets** - Aggregate statistics over time periods
- **Hero cards** - Highlight a single standout achievement

## Periods

Brag sheets can cover different time periods:

### Weekly

Summarize the past 7 days of activity:

- Total QSOs, bands, modes
- Activations completed
- New DXCC entities worked
- Streak status

### Monthly

Summarize the past 30 days:

- All weekly metrics aggregated
- Month-over-month comparison
- Top contacts by distance

### All-Time

Your complete operating history:

- Lifetime QSO count
- Total DXCC entities
- Total states/provinces
- Parks activated and hunted
- All-time records (longest distance, most QSOs in a day, etc.)

## Presets

Brag sheet presets customize which statistics are highlighted based on your operating style:

### General

A balanced overview:
- QSO count and rate
- Band and mode distribution
- Distance statistics
- DXCC and state counts

### DXer

Focused on DX achievement:
- DXCC entity count and list
- Longest distance contacts
- Continent breakdown
- Band-by-entity matrix
- Azimuthal map projection

### QRP

Highlights low-power efficiency:
- Power output prominently displayed
- Watts-per-mile metric
- Distance achievements relative to power
- Equipment details (emphasizing QRP rig)

### Activator

POTA/SOTA/WWFF focused:
- Parks activated count
- Activation success rate
- QSOs per activation average
- Total unique parks
- Program award progress

### Hunter

POTA/SOTA hunter statistics:
- Parks worked count
- Hunters per day rate
- States with parks worked
- Favorite activators (most contacts with)

## Customizable Statistics

### Stat Categories

Each brag sheet can include statistics from these categories:

| Category | Available Stats |
|----------|----------------|
| **Activity** | QSO count, QSOs/hr rate, operating hours, session count |
| **Geography** | DXCC entities, states, grid squares, max distance, avg distance |
| **Equipment** | Radio, antenna, power, key/mic |
| **Programs** | Parks activated, parks hunted, summits, WWFF refs |
| **Modes** | Mode distribution, CW WPM stats |
| **Bands** | Band distribution, band-by-mode matrix |
| **Conditions** | SFI, K-index, weather at time of operation |
| **Streaks** | Current streak, best streak, streak type |

### Selecting Stats

From the share card preview:

1. Tap **Customize**
2. Toggle individual statistics on/off
3. Drag to reorder
4. Preview updates in real time

## Hero Cards

Hero cards highlight a single standout achievement:

- **Longest DX contact** with distance, callsign, and map
- **Best activation** by QSO count or rate
- **Streak milestone** (e.g., "100 Days On Air")
- **New DXCC entity** with country flag and contact details
- **QRP record** with watts-per-mile achievement

Hero cards use a large-format layout with the achievement prominently displayed and supporting context around it.

### Auto-Generated Hero Cards

Carrier Wave automatically generates hero cards when notable achievements are detected:

- A toast notification offers to create the card
- Tap to preview and share
- Cards are also accessible from the Activity feed

## Map Projections

Brag sheets include map visualizations. Two projection options are available:

### Flat Map (Mercator)

- Standard rectangular world map
- QSO locations shown as dots with geodesic arcs
- Best for global coverage visualization
- Default for most brag sheets

### Azimuthal Map

- Centered on your station location
- All distances from center are proportional
- True bearings from center to any point
- Best for DXers wanting to show directional coverage
- Antenna pattern overlay option

### Selecting Projection

Tap the map on the share card preview to toggle between flat and azimuthal projections. The selected projection persists for future cards.

## Professional Statistician Mode

When enabled in Settings -> POTA -> Professional Statistician Mode, brag sheets gain additional visualizations:

### Additional Charts

- **Band distribution chart** (pie or bar)
- **QSO rate over time** line graph
- **Cumulative distance CDF**
- **Cumulative timing CDF**
- **RST histograms** (R, S, T components separately)
- **Distance and timing box plots**

### Additional Metrics

- **Statistical summary** (mean, median, std dev, percentiles)
- **Peak rate badges** ("Best 15 minutes" highlighting)
- **Entity count tables**
- **Band-mode cross-tabulation**

## Share Card Generation

### Preview

Before sharing, a preview sheet shows:

- The generated card image at full resolution
- **Customize** button to adjust content
- **Map projection** toggle
- **ShareLink** button for the iOS share sheet
- **Save to Photos** button

### Output Format

Share cards are generated as PNG images:

- **Resolution:** 2x for Retina displays
- **Aspect ratio:** 16:9 (landscape) for social media compatibility
- **File size:** Typically 200-500 KB
- **Color scheme:** Matches your selected theme (dark/light)

### Sharing Options

Via the iOS share sheet:

- Messages
- Email
- AirDrop
- Twitter/X, Mastodon, Instagram
- Save to Photos
- Save to Files
- Copy to clipboard
